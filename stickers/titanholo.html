Titan Holo Katowice 2014 US$ 62 mil (R$ 311 mil)

A rare Counter-Strike: Global Offensive (CS:GO) skin collector paid $500,000 this week for a batch of eight Titan Katowice 2014 holographic stickers, one of the most expensive stickers ever. match. Each one went for US$ 62,500 (R$ 311 thousand), which is the highest price ever paid for a registered Titan holographic sticker. The transfer was made to a buyer in Russia, according to the ohnePixel page, which specializes in tracking shipments of Valve FPS collector's items.
