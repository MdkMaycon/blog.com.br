AWP Dragon Lore - US$ 151 mil (R$ 801 mil)

One of the top skin sales stores in Counter-Strike: Global Offensive (CS:GO) this week reported a sale of an AWP Dragon Lore tagged with four Titan Holo stickers for $151,000. The amount was paid by an anonymous player and represents the largest transfer involving a skin in Valve FPS history. Interestingly, the three most expensive skins ever traded in game history were Dragon Lore.
